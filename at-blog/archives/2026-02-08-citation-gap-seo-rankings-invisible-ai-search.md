# The Citation Gap: Why Your Top-Ranked Content Is Invisible to AI

Only 12% of URLs cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Copilot rank in Google's top 10. Here's why SEO success doesn't translate to AI visibility—and what actually works.

## Key Insights

- The Citation Gap represents the disconnect between Google rankings and AI citations
- AI systems trust third-party validation (earned media) over owned content
- Earned media has become the single highest-value asset for AI visibility
- Performance-based PR aligns with new visibility requirements
