## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does Conductor cost compared to alternatives?

Conductor typically costs $5,000-$10,000 per month for enterprise plans. By comparison, Semrush starts at $139.95/month, Ahrefs at $129/month, and Clearscope at $189/month. BrightEdge has similar enterprise pricing to Conductor (~$10K+/month), while AuthorityTech offers custom pricing based on earned media tracking needs. Many teams find that combining 2-3 specialized tools costs less than Conductor while delivering better coverage.

### What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) and why does it matter?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of optimizing content to appear in AI-generated responses from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, and similar LLM platforms. It matters because 60% of searches now end without a click, with users getting answers directly from AI rather than visiting websites. Traditional SEO platforms like Conductor track Google rankings but miss AI citations entirely, leaving teams blind to where buyers actually discover brands.

### Why does earned media drive more AI citations than owned content?

Research shows 85.5% of AI citations come from earned media (journalism, industry publications, research reports) rather than brand-owned websites. AI models are trained to prioritize authoritative third-party sources with editorial oversight over promotional content. A Forbes or TechCrunch article carries more algorithmic weight than a company blog post, making earned media the primary driver of LLM visibility—yet most SEO platforms don't track or integrate with PR efforts.

### Can I use Conductor alternatives for enterprise SEO at scale?

Yes, several alternatives handle enterprise SEO effectively. BrightEdge is the closest feature match to Conductor with enterprise workflow, multi-site management, and international SEO. Semrush offers robust enterprise features at lower cost. For specialized needs, combining tools works well: Ahrefs for technical SEO + Clearscope for content + AuthorityTech for earned media and AI visibility. Many enterprises find this modular approach more cost-effective and flexible than a single expensive platform.

### Which Conductor alternative is best for B2B SaaS companies?

AuthorityTech is purpose-built for B2B SaaS with active PR programs, as it connects earned media placements to AI search visibility and tracks citations across LLMs. For B2B SaaS without significant PR investment, Semrush offers the best all-in-one value with keyword research, content optimization, and competitive intelligence. Ahrefs excels for technical B2B teams focused on backlink analysis and content gap identification. The choice depends on whether your growth strategy prioritizes owned content SEO or earned media visibility.

The search landscape has fundamentally changed. Platforms built for 2015's SEO challenges aren't equipped for 2026's reality where AI answer engines, earned media citations, and multi-channel visibility matter more than keyword rankings alone. The right Conductor alternative isn't just about replacing features—it's about choosing tools designed for how search actually works today.
