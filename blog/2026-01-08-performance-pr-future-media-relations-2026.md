### Why Retainer PR Fails

Traditional PR retainers create three fundamental problems:

  - **No Guarantees:** According to industry analysis, **68% of PR retainers lack placement metrics**. Clients pay $5,000+ per month for "strategy" and "outreach" but receive no guarantee of actual media coverage.

  - **Misaligned Incentives:** Agencies get paid regardless of results. There's no urgency to secure placements when the monthly check arrives either way. This leads to "retainer drift," where agencies start strong but gradually reduce effort as the relationship stabilizes.

  - **Opaque Billing:** Brands have no visibility into how retainer dollars are spent. Is that $5,000 monthly fee securing TechCrunch coverage, or funding internal agency overhead? The retainer model makes it impossible to know.

These flaws explain why **70% of PR retainer clients churn within 6 months**. Brands waste months and thousands of dollars before realizing the retainer isn't delivering. By the time they switch, they've lost momentum, budget, and trust in PR as a channel.

### Why Performance PR Succeeds

Performance PR solves these problems by flipping the risk and incentive structure:

  - **Guaranteed Results:** Performance PR platforms guarantee Tier 1 placements or no fee. AuthorityTech secures Forbes, TechCrunch, or WSJ coverage, or you don't pay.

  - **Aligned Incentives:** When agencies only get paid for placements, they focus exclusively on securing coverage. This creates urgency and accountability that retainers can't match.

  - **Transparent Metrics:** Every placement is tracked with clear ROI metrics. You know exactly what you're paying for and what outcomes you're getting.

Research shows that **performance PR delivers 3x better ROI than retainer models**. The reason is simple: you only pay for results, eliminating wasted spend on retainers that deliver zero placements.
