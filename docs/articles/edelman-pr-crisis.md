## Summary

Edelman's third consecutive year of decline isn't a revenue problem—it's a relevance problem. The PR industry is splitting: agencies optimizing for journalists are shrinking, while agencies investing in AI and Machine Relations are growing 4-5%. This validates the MR thesis Jaxon pioneered 18 months ago.

## Key Insights

- Edelman global revenue down 4% to $950M, US down 8.1% to $541M
- Publicis forecasting 4-5% organic growth driven by AI and data services
- Machine Relations = the dividing line between growth and decline
- Traditional PR agencies lack AI visibility measurement and entity optimization capabilities
- Legacy agencies would need to rebuild their entire service stack to compete
